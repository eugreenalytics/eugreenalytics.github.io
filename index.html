About euGreenalytics

Independent analysis for Europe's energy transition

euGreenalytics is an independent platform dedicated to analysing and visualising energy data, with a special focus on developments across Europe. The mission is to transform complex datasets into clear, accessible and actionable insights — from installed capacity trends to electricity markets, technology comparisons, and system flexibility.

The aim is to support better decision-making — from industry professionals to curious citizens — through clear, unbiased and well-structured information. Reflecting years of experience working with energy datasets, photovoltaic technologies and electricity market dynamics.

Consulting support may be available for organisations needing tailored data analysis or custom dashboards.
